Mutini

Mutini was a web mobile app that me and my teammates made for the Reroute/SF hackathon. In about 1.5 days we set out to build an app that would allow users to see if their friends are on their bus and give feedback to the MTA while they were at it. We spend so much time on our phones as we wait for the bus, why not make use of that. In order to get there, we made a transit app where you can find out the bus times of the nearest bus stop and see the bus move on a map first. Being able to see the bus move was a key part of our transit app, so riders can see exactly where the bus is. We didn't have time to build everything that we wanted, but we did win 2nd place in the hackathon.
