what she does

Hi, I am Angel Kittiyachavalit

and I am a designer and developer currently working as the Designer in Residence at Code for America.

I'm a visual and UX designer-developer living in the city of San Francisco, CA. I started designing just three years ago and I’ve been coding here and there for a little over a year. It’s been a great journey with many more things to learn. I’m really excited to have the opportunity to have a career doing what I love.

Before jumping into design, I studied Economics and loved Behavorial Economics in particular. I have not lost that love for it and have been able to apply what I have learned to user experience. I like using both my right and left brain to create order out of chaos especially for good.
